Sergio Padilla Regules

Sergio is an undergraduate student in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science at Tufts University. He leads the Level 1 Rocket program at the Tufts Rocketry Club, guiding and teaching undergraduate students how to build their first rocket. He is also part of the Tufts Swim Club and Tufts Racing Team, working with the ergonomics team. Previously, he has been a learning assistant in PHY-0011: General Physics I and a math and science tutor. He has taught and designed a water bottle rocket activity as part of the EMBRACE program, teaching middle-school students about engineering, aerodynamics, and physics. He has also led and planned TECHO constructions where wooden houses and waste management systems have been implemented in marginalized communities.
